About Kumbhan

Kumbhan is a vibrant Gujarati village situated just 9 km from Mahuva (our closest town and tehsil HQ) and about 100 km from the district seat of Bhavnagar. Officially recognised as a gram panchayat, it serves as a self‑governing hub for the surrounding rural area.

According to the 2011 Census, Kumbhan spans 1,064 hectares, supports a population of 5,426 across 857 households, and balances tradition with progress through strong community initiatives, agriculture, and local enterprise.
