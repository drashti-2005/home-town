Masjid-e-Noor

Main Bazaar Road, Kumbhan

A sacred place for Muslims to perform Salah (namaz) and seek spiritual closeness to Allah.

Masjid-e-Noor serves not only as a place of prayer but also as a center for learning, unity, and devotion. It plays a vital role in community guidance, religious education, and brings people together during special occasions like Ramadan and Eid. Through daily prayers, Friday gatherings, and Islamic teachings, it strengthens faith and fosters brotherhood within the Muslim community.
